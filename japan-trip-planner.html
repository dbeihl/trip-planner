Methodology

  • All hotel rates are live, date-locked quotes for the actual stay dates (Nov 2026), pulled directly from booking platforms — not seasonal floor rates.
  • Every alternative hotel holds a private double/twin room, ≥8.0 guest rating, and the same neighborhood (or one stop away) as the currently-quoted property.
  • Transport fares are current published prices (JR, Odakyu, Nankai, Kintetsu) plus representative private-transfer quotes from real booking platforms (Klook, Viator, Welcome Pickups).
  • International flights ARE now included in the grand total, as researched representative Nov-2026 round-trip fares (per the Flights section) — these are estimates, not the date-locked quotes used for hotels. The Kensington reference comparison stays ground-only, since that quote also excluded airfare.
  • Excluded from every total: Japan's ~10% accommodation tax (varies by city/hotel), meals outside included breakfasts, and activity/entrance fees.
  • Kyoto→Osaka and Osaka→Kansai legs show public-transit cost only — private car quotes for those specific legs weren't part of this research pass.
